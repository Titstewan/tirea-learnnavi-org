Na'vi Sounds

Kaltxì ma frapo. I know this isn't really grammar, but I feel like it can still be very useful to readers like you out there. So this post contains recordings of the sounds of the Na'vi language. So to practice your pronunciation, click the play button and listen, then try to go along and such.
